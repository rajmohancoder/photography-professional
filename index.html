Welcome to Skyline Capture. Based in Chennai with over 8 years of experience capturing the world's quiet beauty, our work spans portrait, landscape, street, and event photography.

I believe every frame tells a story. My approach is rooted in patience, natural light, and finding the extraordinary in ordinary moments. Whether it's the golden hour over Marina Beach or a candid smile at a wedding, I strive to preserve emotions that words cannot express.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring hidden trails, experimenting with darkroom techniques, or mentoring aspiring photographers in the local community.

Behind the Lens

Photography Tips & Insights

Technique

Mastering the Golden Hour

The hour after sunrise and before sunset offers the most flattering, warm light. Position your subject facing the light source and use a wide aperture to create soft, ethereal backgrounds that elevate any portrait or landscape.

5 min read
Equipment

Essential Gear for Every Shoot

You don't need every lens on the market. A versatile 35mm prime, a sturdy tripod, and a reliable reflector can handle 90% of shoots. Invest in quality glass over camera bodies -- your lenses will outlast several generations of sensors.

4 min read
Composition

Breaking the Rule of Thirds

While the rule of thirds is a great starting point, centering your subject can create powerful symmetry and tension. Experiment with leading lines, negative space, and frame-within-frame compositions to develop your own visual signature.

6 min read
Video Insight

The Art of Seeing Light

Photography is not about the camera -- it's about understanding light. In this short breakdown, learn how to read natural light, identify quality vs. direction, and use shadows as a creative tool to add depth and drama to every frame you capture.
